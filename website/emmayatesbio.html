By Emma Yates

Emma Yates is an Australian born, New York based designer who specializes in unconventional bridal wear. Born to parents who never legally married, but still experienced a chaotic divorce, Emma aims to discuss and redefine ideas around marriage in her work. She draws much of her inspiration from her childhood spent between Sydney and Australian outback. Emma is passionate about supporting Australian farmers and works directly with farmers in the Hilltops Region to source her raw materials, before processing them to create looks for the modern bride.

Emma has worked alongside a number of industry leaders including receiving sponsorships from Swarovski and Sophie Hallette for her Fall/Winter 2018 collection.

In 2019 Emma was named as a finalist for the Parsons School of Fashion Luxarity Lane Crawford award.
